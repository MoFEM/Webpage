Detailed Description

Projection of edge entities with one mid-node on hierarchical basis.

Examples
adolc_plasticity.cpp, approx_sphere.cpp, dynamic_first_order_con_law.cpp, elasticity.cpp, elasticity_mixed_formulation.cpp, forces_and_sources_testing_edge_element.cpp, forces_and_sources_testing_flat_prism_element.cpp, hcurl_curl_operator.cpp, hdiv_divergence_operator.cpp, navier_stokes.cpp, nonlinear_dynamics.cpp, plastic.cpp, Remodeling.cpp, simple_contact.cpp, simple_contact_thermal.cpp, simple_interface.cpp, tensor_divergence_operator.cpp, test_jacobian_of_simple_contact_element.cpp, testing_jacobian_of_hook_element.cpp, and testing_jacobian_of_hook_scaled_with_density_element.cpp.
