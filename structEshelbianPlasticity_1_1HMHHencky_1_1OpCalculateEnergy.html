Detailed Description

Calculate energy density for Hencky material model.

\[ \Psi(\log{\mathbf{U}}) = \frac{1}{2} U_{IJ} D_{IJKL} U_{KL} = \frac{1}{2} U_{IJ} T_{IJ} \]

where \(T_{IJ} = D_{IJKL} U_{KL}\) is a a Hencky stress.
